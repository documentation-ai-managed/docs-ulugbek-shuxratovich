## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- No prior installation required â€“ everything runs in your browser

<Callout kind="info">
  Ulugbek Shuxratovich is a cloud-based documentation platform. You access it directly via `https://app.ulugbekshuxratovich.com`.
</Callout>

## Step 1: Register Your Account

Follow these steps to create your free account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://app.ulugbekshuxratovich.com/signup`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button prominently displayed on the homepage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Fill in the form with:

    | Field       | Example                  |
    |-------------|--------------------------|
    | Email       | you@example.com         |
    | Password    | A strong password (8+ chars) |
    | Full Name   | Your Name               |

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Ulugbek Shuxratovich.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Project

Once logged in, set up your documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Dashboard Overview" icon="layout">
    You'll land on the dashboard. Click **New Project** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Project Details" icon="plus">
    Enter:
    - Project Name: `My First Docs`
    - Description: `Organize my project documentation here`

    Select **Public** or **Private** visibility, then click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Create Your First Document

Add content to your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your project, click **New Page** and name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Content" icon="pen-tool">
    Paste this starter template:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Introduction
    description: Welcome to my docs
    ---

    ## Hello World

    This is your first document!
    ````

    ````markdown
    # Introduction

    Welcome to my docs.

    ## Hello World

    This is your first document!
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Save" icon="eye">
    Toggle the preview pane. Click **Save** then **Publish** to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You've created your first documentation space. Your site is now live at a unique URL.
</Callout>
