## Getting Started

To begin managing your documentation:

1. Sign in at `https://ulugbekshuxratovich.com`
2. Click "New Project" on the dashboard
3. Enter your project name and description
4. Click "Create"

Your first documentation project will appear in the Projects list. Click on it to start adding, editing, or organizing your docs.

For more advanced options, explore the settings menu inside your project.
