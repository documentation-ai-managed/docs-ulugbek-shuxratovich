## Add a Documentation Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Select your desired project from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click `Add Page`">
    You'll find the `Add Page` button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the details">
    Enter a title, description, and select a section (if needed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save". Your new page is ready for editing and collaboration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
