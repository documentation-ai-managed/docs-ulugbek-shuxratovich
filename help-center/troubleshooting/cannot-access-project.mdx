---
title: Cannot Access Project
description: Resolve access issues to your documentation project.
---

## Symptoms

- Cannot open your project
- Error: "Access denied" or "Not found"

## Possible Causes

- Not signed in
- Project permissions issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with correct account">
    Make sure you are logged in to the account that owns the project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Go to your project's settings and verify your user role is "Editor" or higher.
  </Step>
</Steps>
